Transform your hair care routine with Hey Hun's innovative Leave in Conditioner and Hair Oil Set, expertly designed for those struggling with dry, damaged, or frizzy hair. This dual-action system combines the deep penetrating power of a moisturising leave in spray conditioner with the protective benefits of a lightweight, non-greasy hair oil. Whether you have curly, straight, thick, or fine hair, this unisex treatment delivers professional salon results at home. What makes this set exceptional is the carefully selected blend of natural ingredients. Argan oil, renowned for its fatty acid content, provides intensive moisture and strengthens hair from root to tip. Honey extract acts as a natural humectant, locking in hydration to combat dryness and brittleness. Shea butter smooths the hair cuticle, reducing frizz and adding brilliant shine. Unlike harsh chemical treatments, our sulphate-free and paraben-free formula gently nourishes without stripping natural oils or fading colour, making it ideal for colour-treated and chemically processed hair. Using this treatment is simple and flexible. For daily styling, spray the leave in conditioner on damp hair after washing, focusing on mid-lengths and ends, then apply a small amount of hair oil to seal in moisture and add shine. For intensive repair, apply generously to dry hair before bed as an overnight treatment, allowing the nourishing ingredients to work while you sleep. The leave in conditioner detangles and prepares hair for styling, while the oil provides heat protection and finishing touches. Together, they address split ends, dry scalp, breakage, and frizz while promoting softness, manageability, and healthy growth.
