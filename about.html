About: us

A family-run register of beauty, grocery & household goods — sourced from trusted British brands and delivered with care.

Welcome to Shopcienty.

Shopcienty's a small London register for beauty, grocery & household goods — built on a simple idea: the everyday essentials households depend upon deserve the same care a sommelier brings to wine.

Why Shopcienty exists

The register was founded to replace endless scrolling through thousands of dubious listings with one considered catalogue. We partner directly with trusted British and international brands — no filler, no mystery labels, just honest goods, properly described, dispatched promptly and delivered free to your door.

What makes us different

Shopcienty isn't a marketplace. It's a register, kept by hand. We stock only what we would keep in our own cupboards — and the list grows slowly, one considered entry at a time.

Plainly described

No inflated copy, no mystery labels, no fine print. What you read on the page is what arrives on the step — properly packaged, and usually a day or two sooner than you expected.

Our promise

Every product in the register is chosen for quality, value, and reliability. Shopping for household essentials should be straightforward and satisfying — not an ordeal of comparison tables and uncertain reviews.

Returned without protest

Thirty days to change your mind, no script, no reasoning required. If something isn't right, our returns process is designed to be as painless as possible. That's not a policy — it's a principle.

The bottom line

Shopcienty's the register to check first. Start there, and chances are you'll find what you need at a price and with a service that feels right — and a little more British than the rest of the internet.
